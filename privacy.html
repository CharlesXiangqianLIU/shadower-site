隐私政策 · Shadower / 跟读者

最后更新：2026-06-26

Shadower（中文名「跟读者」，下称"本 App"）非常重视你的隐私。本 App 的核心原则是：所有处理都在你的设备本地完成，我们不收集、不上传、不存储你的任何个人数据。

我们收集的数据

没有。本 App 不收集任何个人信息。你导入的音视频文件、生成的课文、你的录音，全部只保存在你自己的 iPhone 上，绝不上传到任何服务器。

本地处理

  • 语音识别 / 转写：使用苹果设备端（on-device）语音识别，全程离线，内容不离开你的设备。
  • 录音：仅用于跟读练习与发音对比，保存在本机，你可随时删除。
  • 麦克风：仅在你主动录音时使用。

第三方

本 App 不含任何广告、分析、追踪或第三方 SDK。

订阅

Pro 订阅通过 Apple App Store 处理。相关支付与交易由 Apple 管理，受 Apple 隐私政策 约束；我们不会接触到你的支付信息。

儿童

本 App 适合所有年龄段使用，且不收集任何数据。

Privacy Policy · Shadower

Last updated: 2026-06-26

Shadower takes your privacy seriously. Our core principle: everything is processed locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or store any of your personal data.

Data We Collect

None. Shadower collects no personal information. The media you import, the lessons generated, and your recordings stay only on your iPhone and are never uploaded to any server.

On-Device Processing

  • Speech recognition / transcription: uses Apple's on-device speech recognition. It runs entirely offline; content never leaves your device.
  • Recordings: used only for shadowing practice and pronunciation comparison, stored locally, and deletable anytime.
  • Microphone: used only while you actively record.

Third Parties

Shadower contains no advertising, analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs.

Subscriptions

Pro subscriptions are handled by the Apple App Store. Payments and transactions are managed by Apple under the Apple Privacy Policy. We never see your payment information.

Children

Shadower is suitable for all ages and collects no data.
